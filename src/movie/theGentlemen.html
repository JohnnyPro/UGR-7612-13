The Gentlemen is a well constructed story that starts slow but that will end in a rollercoaster of emotions. With nice twists, good suspense and action, and impressive performances from all the cast really cements this movie as one for the ages. A 2 hours long British crime drama, which doesn't take itself too serious. All in all very enjoyable classic Guy Ritchie film.

Title The Gentlemen
Director Guy Ritchie
Cast Matthew McConaughey, Charlie Hunnam, Michelle Dockery, ...
Score 9/10
