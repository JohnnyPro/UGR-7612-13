Shutter Island is a visually intriguing, simplistic and absolutely phenomenal. The story is kept simplistic enough so it doesn't get absurd, but allows for an ending which you probably won't see coming. The film doesn't go for cheap thrills, so although you will be on the edge of your seat you won't get needlessly scared. The film uses everything at its disposal from breathtaking scenery, to detailed laid-out shots, and to actors at their finest to completely engross you in the film

Title Shutter Island
Director Martin Scorsese
Cast Leonardo DiCaprio, Mark Ruffalo, Ben Kingsley...
Score 9/10
