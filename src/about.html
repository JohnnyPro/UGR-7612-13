Who we are

MovieReview is the first website where moviegoers review movies instead of film critics. As the divide between critics and audience reviews increases, We decided to make a review website dedicated to audience opinions. Anyone can ask to be a reviewer(yes, even you), If you are interested you can apply here.

What we do

We, at MovieReview, help our users by:

  • reviewing movies in the eyes of the audience to give a more grounded and relevant review.
  • We watch movies to help users decide which movies are worth watching and which ones are a waste of time.
  • We provide reviews on trending movies for users to value if they are worth going to the cinema for
