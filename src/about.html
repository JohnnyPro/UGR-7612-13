Who we are

Movie Review is the first website where movie goers review movies instead of film critics. As the divide between critics and audience review increases We decided to make a review website fully dedicated to audiences opinions of movies to give a more grounded and relevant review to our users. Anyone can ask to be a reviewer(yes, even you) by applying here.
